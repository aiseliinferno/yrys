Education

I am 29. I was born in raised in the Aksy region in village Zhylgyn. Zhylgyn is the second-closest village to a beautiful lake, Sary-chelek, after the Arkyt village

After school, I came to Bishkek to study at university and graduated in 2013 from Kyrgyz Turkish Manas University in Engineering of Food Science major.

I loved my program because it included all types of chemical lessons that ever existed, which I hated at school. So, I should have loved it, as I didn't have other choices.

But I truly enjoyed lessons such as thermodynamics, process control, manufacture design, biotechnology, sensory analysis.

Also, we had the chance to visit food companies such as Coca- Cola, Kyrgyz Konyagy, Akun, Yashar, sugar productions.

Except for the study, I was part of the University Dance team "Manastyn Kyzdary".We were performing in all University cultural programs. Sometimes we were dancing at weddings and getting money for that).

Work life

After graduation, I started my working life at the company "ejootrade". Here I learned first about how to build customer relationships and how to sell products. Also, I was part of an amazing team.
In 2015 I moved to Istanbul, and here it started my career as a food engineer in the corporate field.

Here I learned a lot about corporate business, life, strategy, etc. I had the chance to visit a lot of food productions in different countries, which widens my horizon.
